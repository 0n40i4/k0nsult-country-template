Status legend

  • DATA — the table is filled with real rows taken directly from the source dataset.
  • GAP — field/entry without hard evidence in the source; labelled instead of invented.
  • implementation / pilot — a tool launched or tested, confirmed by a public statement or document.
  • team / coordination / governance — a team, coordinator or policy exists, without a fully deployed tool.
  • strategy / concept / analysis — a direction-setting document, report or expert study without production deployment.
  • no hard evidence / to verify — no public confirmation; control entry.
claim ≤ proof. Entries with a low readiness score and the status "no hard evidence / to verify" are not presented as deployments — they mean there is no public confirmation within the examined scope and require a freedom-of-information request or a register query. They are not presented as facts.
